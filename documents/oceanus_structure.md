# Oceanus Website Structure Analysis
**Source URL:** [oceanusthelabel.com](https://oceanusthelabel.com/)
**Date:** 2026-02-01

This document outlines the visual structure and content hierarchy of the Oceanus homepage to serve as a reference for the Swimdays digital flagship.

## 1. Navbar & Header
**Goal:** Utility and Brand Identity.

*   **Announcement Bar (Top)**
    *   Auto-scrolling text ticker.
    *   *Content Examples:* "Free U.S. Shipping on orders $600+", "Discover the new Disney | Oceanus Collection".
*   **Main Header (Sticky/Fixed)**
    *   **Left:** 
        *   **Billboard / Mobile Menu Icon**: A unique icon (billboard style) or hamburger menu that opens the side navigation.
    *   **Center:** 
        *   **Logo**: "OCEANUS" wordmark, centered.
    *   **Right (Utility Icons):**
        *   **Search**: Magnifying glass icon.
        *   **Store Locator**: Pin icon.
        *   **Account/Wishlist**: User profile icon.
        *   **Bag/Cart**: Shopping bag icon with item count.
        *   **Currency Selector**: Dropdown (e.g., INR, USD, GBP).

## 2. Hero Section
**Goal:** High-impact brand storytelling.

*   **Type:** Full-screen Image Slider / Carousel.
*   **Content:**
    *   High-editorial lifestyle photography (e.g., Disney Collaboration, Resort Wear).
    *   **Overlay Text:** Minimalist.
    *   **CTA Button:** "Shop Now" (often centered).
*   **Navigation:** Dot indicators and L/R arrows.

## 3. Product & Collection Sections (Stacked)
**Goal:** Immediate product discovery and categorization. The homepage relies on a vertical stack of carousels.

### Block A: Key Collaboration / Highlight
*   **Title:** e.g., "Disney | Oceanus"
*   **Format:** Product Carousel.
*   **Card Elements:** 
    *   Product Image (Hover: Lifestyle/Alternate view).
    *   "Quick Add" button (overlay or visible).
    *   Name (e.g., "Ariel Dress").
    *   Price.
    *   Badges: "Bestseller".

### Block B: Best Sellers
*   **Title:** "Best Sellers"
*   **Format:** Product Carousel of top-performing items mixed from all categories.

### Block C: Category Focus - Dresses
*   **Title:** "Dresses"
*   **Format:** Carousel highlighting evening and resort dresses.

### Block D: Category Focus - Bags & Accessories
*   **Title:** "Bags" / "Jewellery"
*   **Format:** Carousel showing non-apparel items (e.g., beaded bags, earrings).

## 4. Visual Navigation Grid
**Goal:** Visual department browsing.

*   **Layout:** 4-Column Grid (Desktop) / stacked on Mobile.
*   **Content:** Large lifestyle images representing core categories.
*   **Labels:** Large Serif Typography Overlay.
    1.  **[DRESSES]** - "Shop Now"
    2.  **[SWIMSUITS]** - "Shop Now"
    3.  **[BIKINIS]** - "Shop Now"
    4.  **[CO-ORDS]** - "Shop Now"

## 5. Engagement & Social Protocol
**Goal:** Community building and trust.

*   **Instagram Section:**
    *   **Header:** "Follow us on Instagram"
    *   **Subtext:** "Tag #OCEANUS to be featured"
    *   **CTA:** "Follow Us" button.
*   **Marquee Banner:**
    *   Fast-scrolling text divider.
    *   *Content:* "Worldwide Delivery", "Shipping Info", Brand Values.

## 6. Newsletter Capture
**Goal:** Lead generation.

*   **Header:** "Sign up and get 10% off your first order."
*   **Inputs:** 
    *   Email Address.
    *   Phone Number (SMS Marketing).
*   **Button:** High-contrast "SIGN ME UP!" button.

## 7. Footer
**Goal:** Comprehensive navigation.

*   **Layout:** 4-Column Text Links.
*   **Columns:**
    1.  **About:** About Us, Press, Blogs, Careers, Collaborations.
    2.  **Customer Service:** FAQs, Delivery & Returns, Policies, Terms.
    3.  **Contact:** Email link, WhatsApp link.
    4.  **Socials:** List of social media links (IG, TikTok, Pinterest).
*   **Special Feature:**
    *   **Oceanus Playlist:** "LISTEN THE OCEANUS PLAYLIST" with a "PLAY ON SPOTIFY" button.
